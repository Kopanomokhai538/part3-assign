About Food Factory

Food Factory is a fast food restaurant based in Bayswater, Bloemfontein.

Our mission is to provide quality meals at affordable prices while maintaining good customer service.

Our Vision

We aim to become one of the most popular fast food restaurants in Bloemfontein.

Services

Our Food

We offer a variety of meals including burgers, chicken wings, seafood and sides. All meals are prepared fresh daily.
